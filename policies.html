Community standards

Terms, Policies, and Conditions

These standards protect academic integrity and keep the platform useful.

Students may NOT share instances of their past projects, essays, or long-term academic endeavors relevant to a specific class. If a student attempts to share these materials, they will be flagged by our team and will be removed from the platform. The contributor may also be prevented from sharing materials in the future.

Inappropriate or irrelevant uploads of topics may not be contributed to the platform. Only when a topic is relevant to a subject or when a specific explanation of a topic is requested by a student may a video or materials on that topic be uploaded.

Each upload undergoes a vetting process to ensure compliance with these terms, policies, and conditions. Let's work together to make this platform a helpful resource, medium, and connection between students in MCPS.
